Traffic Time

Fast ETAs for iOS, iPadOS, and macOS

   

Traffic Time makes it easy to view and share exactly how long it will take to reach all your favorite destinations.

  • iMessage app allows you to quickly add your ETA to any text message
  • Home Screen Widgets to keep your ETAs at your fingertips
  • Easily visualize the travel times from your exact location with the Map View
  • Optionally show your ETA with extra time added to account for walking to your car, parking, etc.
  • View all of your destinations in a single list with the fastest route shown
  • Automatically sync your destinations between the Mac, iPhone, and iPad apps
  • Support for dark mode and iCloud syncing
